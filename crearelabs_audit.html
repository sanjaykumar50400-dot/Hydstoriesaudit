Common Reasons Accounts Get Blocked

REASON 01
Policy Violations in Ad Copy
Misleading claims, prohibited language, or restricted content in ads. Even small words like "guaranteed" can trigger Meta's automated system. 3 consecutive rejected ads = automatic disable.
REASON 02
Payment Method Issues
40% of all disabled Meta accounts are payment-related. A failed card charge, expired card, or billing address mismatch triggers automatic account hold.
REASON 03
Unverified Business Manager
Meta requires Business Verification (govt ID or business documents) for accounts that want to run ads. Without this, accounts get flagged as suspicious.
REASON 04
New Account — Sudden High Spend
New ad accounts that immediately spend large amounts look fraudulent to Meta's algorithm. Spend should be scaled gradually from $5/day upward.
REASON 05
Suspicious Login Activity
Logging in from different locations, multiple IP addresses, or devices without 2FA enabled triggers Meta's security system automatically.
REASON 06
Automated System Error
95% of Meta account restrictions are triggered by automated bots, not humans. Many are false positives that are resolved through the proper appeal process.

Crearelabs 5-Step Recovery Plan

STEP 01 — DIAGNOSE
Identify the Exact Reason
  • Go to Meta Business Support Home
  • Check "Account Status Overview"
  • Read the exact violation notice
  • Identify which asset is blocked: Business Manager, Ad Account, or Page
STEP 02 — FIX ROOT CAUSE
Resolve Before Appealing
  • If payment: Add new valid credit card, clear balance
  • If policy: Remove or edit violating ad creative
  • If identity: Enable Two-Factor Authentication
  • If verification: Submit govt ID or business license
STEP 03 — APPEAL
Submit Professional Appeal
  • Business Support Home → Select account → "Request Review"
  • Include: Business Manager ID, Ad Account ID, date of restriction
  • Write clear appeal explaining corrective steps taken
  • Attach: Business license, govt ID, compliance screenshots
  • Must submit within 180 days of restriction
STEP 04 — MONITOR
Track & Follow Up
  • Monitor "Cases" section in Business Support Home
  • Most cases reviewed within 48–72 hours
  • Do NOT submit multiple appeals — delays the process
  • Be ready to provide additional info if Meta requests it
STEP 05 — REBUILD
If Denied — Escalate or Rebuild
  • Re-appeal with new evidence (not same appeal twice)
  • Contact Meta Support team directly via help menu
  • As last resort: New Business Manager (only after exhausting all appeals)
  • Crearelabs sets up proper verified account structure from scratch
ONGOING — PREVENTION
Stop It Happening Again
  • All ad copy reviewed against Meta's 120+ policy parameters before launch
  • Maintain 2–3 payment methods from different issuers
  • Scale ad spend gradually: $5 → $10 → $20/day
  • Weekly Account Quality Dashboard checks
  • Business Verification completed and maintained
